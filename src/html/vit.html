VIT Exam Overview

Exam Pattern
VITEEE is conducted in computer-based test mode. The exam consists of:
- Physics: 40 questions
- Chemistry: 40 questions
- Mathematics or Biology: 40 questions
- English: 5 questions
Each question carries 1 mark with no negative marking.
Important
Preparation Tips
  • • Focus on NCERT concepts first
  • • Practice numerical problems daily
  • • Take regular mock tests
  • • Analyze your weak areas
  • • Maintain a formula notebook
